Stephanie Wu is an illustrator and designer based in Boston and Providence. She is currently pursing a BFA in Illustration at Rhode Island School of Design. She is interested in the intersection of illustration, animation, and design.

Stephanie designs for RISD IDSA, art directs for Brown Political Review, and illustrates for Brown Daily Herald post- and The College Hill Independent. Previously at Artists For Humanity. She enjoys building calluses from rock climbing, the Boston Public Library, and public transportation. This website was created by Stephanie!
