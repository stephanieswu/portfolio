Stephanie is an illustrator and designer based in Boston and Providence. She is currently pursing a BFA in Illustration at Rhode Island School of Design. She is deeply interested in observing others and herself. Much of her work stems from personal experiences.

Outside of the class room, she works with Brown Political Review, Brown Daily Herald Post, and RISD IDSA. When she has nothing to do catch her watching Mad Men and trying to boulder as much as possible.
